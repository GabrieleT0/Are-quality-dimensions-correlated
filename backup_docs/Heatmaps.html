Each cell of the matrix, contains the Spearman correlation coefficient representing the monotonic relationship between the dimension in the row and the dimension in the column. The diagonal elements of the matrix are all equal to 1, as they represent the self-correlation of each dimension. Positive values indicate a direct monotonic relationship, while negative values signify an inverse monotonic relationship between the dimensions. The presence of asterisks next to a correlation value signifies its level of statistical significance (p-value). * indicates p-value < 0.05, ** indicates p-value < 0.01, and *** indicates p < 0.001.
